Rarity: Currency
Coin of Restoration
--------
Stack Size: 8/10

Restores an Afarud Unique item to its past Maraketh form
--------
"Drain away the sands of Time,
restore that which is now mine!"
--------
Right click this item then left click a valid Afarud Unique item to restore it to its Maraketh form.
